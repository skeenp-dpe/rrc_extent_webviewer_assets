Details

This option is for a flow limit of 22,000 ML/d at Wagga Wagga. The mapping shows the upper limit of inundation at this flow rate (light blue) plus an inundation extent that would be more typically experienced when environmental flow releases are made (dark blue). Two extents are shown to demonstrate that inundation outcomes associated with investigated flow releases will be variable in response to catchment conditions and typically less than the upper limit.
